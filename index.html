Cole Solari

Lifelong Learner

Fun facts.


Raised in the small railroad town of Lincoln, California. Our high school mascot was the Fighting Zebra!


Looking shaggy and uncharacteristically stern while giving a commencement address for Union High School in Camas, WA (2019). I love public speaking!


Served as a full-time missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the Utah Orem Mission (2019-2021). Sharing the gospel is one of my favorite hobbies.


I've been in many bands, choirs and orchestras. Please enjoy one of my favorite choral performances!
